Our Story

From a Small Idea to a Growing Movement

It all started in 2020 when our founder, witnessing the struggles of local farmers and the disconnect between producers and customers, envisioned a platform that could bridge this gap.

What began as a small initiative in one village has now grown into a thriving marketplace connecting over 500 farmers with thousands of families across 50+ cities.

Our journey has been driven by one simple belief: when farmers thrive, communities flourish.
